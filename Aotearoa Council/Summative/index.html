Aotearoa's Council of Wāhine

Aotearoa's Council of Wāhine (ACW) is a national, feminist, membership, movement-support Council working to achieve gender justice and women's human rights in Aotearoa

Kaupapa - Who are we

ACW is an umbrella organisation working towards true gender equality in Aotearoa New Zealand. Established in 1896, we have 13 branches and more than 200 organisations, as well as individuals, as members.

Over the past 126 years we have led and supported many initiatives that have benefited all tāngata Aotearoa/people of New Zealand. We are committing to working in a collaborative, inclusive way – honouring Te Tiriti o Waitangi, being guided by feminism and constantly learning.
